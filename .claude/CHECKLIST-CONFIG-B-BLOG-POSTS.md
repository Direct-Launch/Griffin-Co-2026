## 💡 **KEY POINTS**

✅ **Keyword placement is natural** - Don't force it, use in headings/intro/conclusion  
✅ **Readability first** - Short paragraphs, subheadings, bullet lists  
✅ **Internal links matter** - Link to 2-3 related posts/pages  
✅ **Brand voice consistent** - Match tone from Obsidian writing style guide  
✅ **Yoast is a guide, not a law** - Green light is great but don't sacrifice readability

## ❓ **COMMON QUESTIONS**

**Q: Should I write from scratch or optimize existing content?**  
A: Either works! Optimization (starting with existing) is usually faster (lower cost).

**Q: How many internal links should I include?**  
A: 2-3 per 1000 words. Link naturally within the text, not forced at end.

**Q: What if I'm below Yoast green?**  
A: Check: keyword density (not too low), readability (paragraph length), links. Adjust and recheck.

**Q: Can I use this for guest posts or external blogs?**  
A: Yes! Just adjust: remove internal links to your site, adjust brand tone as needed.

**Q: How long should the post be?**  
A: Depends on topic. Minimum 800 words for SEO. 1200-1800 is ideal for most topics.
