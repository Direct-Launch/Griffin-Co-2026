  1. Count tasks by day
  2. Estimate tokens by task type (or I'll give you numbers)
  3. Fill in WEEKLY TOTAL section
  4. Add brief notes
  5. Save

MONTHLY SUMMARY (Last day of month):
  1. Sum all weekly totals
  2. Calculate cost ($: tokens × $0.003/1000)

  4. Calculate efficiency metrics
  5. Note insights and optimization opportunities
  6. Plan improvements for next month
